Notable quirks worth flagging

Quirk 01
Many deals have zero logged activities

Records have detailed Notes/Comments but num_notes = 0. Reps may be using the Notes field as the sales journal instead of logging activities. Activity reporting will look weak; automation that depends on activity won't trigger.

Ask Katie: "Are reps expected to log activity, or are they mostly using Notes/Comments as the sales journal?"
Quirk 02
Notes/Comments contain gold but are unstructured

"Using Shamrock label" · "Need pricing" · "Already using Perricone" · "Opportunity to convert from Florida's Natural" · "Going no plastic" · "Avendra Partner" · "Grab and go opportunity" · "Using OJ, grapefruit, lemon and lime for bar." This is rich intel — and almost impossible to report on.

Quirk 03
Current-client vs new-client deals feel blurred

Some New Client deals say "Already using Perricone" or "Already pouring Natalie's." These are probably expansion, not new business. A simple "Is this net-new, existing customer expansion, or reactivation?" field would clean this up.

Quirk 04
Trade Show fields exist but are underused

The portal has Trade Show Name and Trade Show Status fields. Comments mention "Meet at show" and "stayed here for show," but those structured fields aren't consistently filled. Event-driven selling looks important — the fields should reflect that.

Quirk 05
"Warning" stage exists but isn't defined

Every pipeline has a Warning stage — powerful if it's automatic. Candidates: no activity in 30 days · close date passed · revenue mismatch · invalid deal stage · sample dropped but no follow-up · Target with no next activity. The ingredients for a risk system are already in the portal.

Ask Katie: "Should Warning become an automated risk stage instead of a manual one?"
